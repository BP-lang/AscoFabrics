Our Story

We Don't Buy From Manufacturers.
We Are One.

Asco started in apparel in the 1950s — manufacturing garments long before most brands in Pakistan existed.

A decade ago, we moved into blended fabric for the local market. Same manufacturing discipline. Same export-grade standards. Just a different product.

We procure yarn and handle everything after — weaving, dyeing, finishing — all in-house. What leaves our factory is exactly what we intended to make.

Seventy years of making things well. Now in the fabric you wear.
